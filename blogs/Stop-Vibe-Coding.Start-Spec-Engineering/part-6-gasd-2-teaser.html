What's Next — A Glimpse at GASD 2.0

Part 6 of 6 · Teaser

One agent, one spec: solved. Ten agents, one codebase: welcome to the next problem.

GASD 1.0 ensures a single AI agent generates consistent, deterministic code. But the future is teams of engineers working alongside fleets of AI agents.

The Coordination Tax

Incompatibilities emerge when multiple agents, each individually correct, produce outputs that don't work together. GASD 2.0 is designed to be the shared contract that keeps fleets of agents aligned.

The Bigger Vision

GASD is working toward becoming the lingua franca of collaborative, human-supervised, multi-agent software engineering.
